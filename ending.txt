And so ended the story of Agent Kelley, one more betrayal at the hands of a man for whom betrayal was second nature.

Publicly, J.E. Vacuumcleaner mourned the loss of his finest agent, while privately continuing his nefarious scheme. In his mind, garlic was the root of all evil, and the world would not be safe until every last clove was gone.

But perhaps there are second chances. No normal human could have survived the hail of bullets, but Agent Kelley was no normal human.

In time, they would be faced with a choice. To confront the past, or to let go and begin again.
